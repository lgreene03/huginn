# NNNN. Short, declarative title

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by [ADR-XXXX](xxxx-...)
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** name(s) or role(s)
- **Related:** links to issues, PRs, or other ADRs

## Context

What is the problem? What is the constraint or force that requires a decision? Two to four short paragraphs. State the problem, not the solution.

If this decision rests on or modifies a steering doc, link it.

## Decision

The decision itself, stated in one or two sentences. No hedging. If you cannot state it crisply, the decision is not made.

A short justification follows: the principles or constraints that drove this choice.

## Consequences

What becomes easier? What becomes harder? What new constraints or obligations does this create? What follow-up work is implied?

Be honest about downsides. ADRs that list only benefits are not credible.

## Alternatives Considered

- **Alternative A.** Why it was rejected.
- **Alternative B.** Why it was rejected.
- **Status quo.** Why doing nothing was rejected.

## References

- Links to prior art, vendor documentation, RFCs, related ADRs.
