# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/). All contributors are expected to uphold its standards.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainer at the email listed in `SECURITY.md`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
